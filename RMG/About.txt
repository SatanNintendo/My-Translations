I attempted to translate this wonderful emulator into Russian, but currently, the emulator doesn't support language switching. So, when that feature is likely implemented, this translation should be a good fit. However, it's important to understand that since there was no built-in support, I did the translation completely blind, without seeing how it would appear. This means it's an untested translation. If I get the chance, I'll definitely test it and correct any issues that arise.

A huge thank you to Rosalie for this excellent emulator!
