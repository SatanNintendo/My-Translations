The Project64 1.6.2 emulator is the best fork I've ever seen. It supports various romhacks and also has the ability to run games at 60 frames per second,
like GoldenEye 007 and many others. I personally love racing games, and titles like Destruction Derby 64 and Roadsters Trophy scroll incredibly smoothly.
Overall, it's a pleasure to play; if you don't like low frame rates, this emulator is definitely for you.

What surprised me even more is that in recent versions, the author has started fixing the audio.
I've never before seen any emulator named Project64 properly emulate sound in Top Gear Rally, but now it does.

Many thanks to the emulator's author, BruceShankleIV, for all the hard work! This is truly a significant contribution to emulator development!
